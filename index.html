My Story

I design for scale — systems that move millions of users and the teams who build for them.

Born in the serenity of Dehradun, shaped by the hustle of Mumbai — I've spent 18 years at the intersection of craft, strategy, and people. I've co-founded startups, led design for some of India's most-used products, built teams from zero, and written about what matters: design, parenting, and the human condition.

In B2B, I've designed for growth marketers, CRM agents, and analysts — turning data-dense enterprise workflows into things people actually want to use. In B2C gaming, I've built high-retention consumer experiences that balance engagement, monetisation, and responsible design.
